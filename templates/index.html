About Us

Trapti Jan Kalyan is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to empowering children and young women through education and skill development. We provide support, guidance, and resources to help individuals build a better future.

Our NGO organizes educational drives and sewing workshops to help young girls become self-reliant and confident. We aim to support all individuals in need and uplift communities through meaningful initiatives.
